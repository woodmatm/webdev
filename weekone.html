This week I learned how to setup my first HTML blog webpage. I learned over 10 new tags that I can use to improve the way my webpages look to my viewers and to help me organize all of my source code. One of the most notible things I learned this week was how to make a webpage form for user input. Learning how you can connect your labels to your inputs within a form will be really good for the future. I want to use what I learned to make a contact page for my own personal portfolio website. I also learned how I can collaborate with other developers using Live Share. It is one of the most responsive collaboration tools I have seen.
